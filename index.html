Roadmap

Phase 1

  • Recruitment of team members with relevant expertise.
  • Define the project's vision, goals, and objectives
  • Conduct market research and analysis
  • Identify the target audience and competition
  • Define the project's tokenomics and revenue model
  • Creation of social media accounts to build a community and engage with potential investors
  • Creation of Telegram community
  • Create Logo and other branding material
  • Begin Development of Website

Phase 2

  • Launch of pre-sale to raise funds and engage with early supporters
  • Building a community by providing regular updates, hosting events and webinars, and answering questions
  • Running promotions, bonuses and referral programs to engage and reward supporters
  • Launch of Website
  • Acquire Partnerships and Social Media Influencers
  • Begin Development of NFTs and Staking platform

Phase 3

  • Public launch of the project and its services
  • Release of NFTs and other digital assets
  • Integration of staking mechanisms to incentivize users to hold and support the currency
  • Expansion of the project's use case by integrating it into relevant platforms and services
  • Continued Marketing Campaign to include News Article releases
  • Continued development and improvement of the project to meet the evolving market demand.

Phase 4

  • Begin development of trading tools
  • Conduct market research to determine the specific needs and demands of the target audience
  • Develop a detailed project plan and scope, including timeline, budget, and resources required
  • Determine the appropriate technology stack for the project
  • Finalize the design and user interface of the trading tools
  • Conduct testing and debugging to ensure that the tools work as expected
  • Launch the trading tools to the market and promote through relevant channels
